# Chapters Sidebar

The **Chapters Sidebar** allows you to navigate and manage the structure of your story.

## Usage
- **Drag and Drop**: Reorder chapters by dragging them.
- **Add**: Click `+` to add a new chapter.
- **Edit**: Click a chapter to open it in the editor.
- **Metadata menu**: Click the 3 dots next to a chapter to open a window to add chapter specific details such as the date its set in.
