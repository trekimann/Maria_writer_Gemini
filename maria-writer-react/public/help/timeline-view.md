# Timeline View

The Timeline View provides a visual representation of your story's events, laid out chronologically.

## Navigation
- **Pan**: Click and drag on the timeline background to move left or right.
- **Zoom**: Use the `+` and `-` buttons in the toolbar, or hold `Ctrl` and scroll your mouse wheel.
- **Reset**: Click the `⟲` button to reset the zoom level.

## Date Controls
- **Fit**: Click "Fit" to automatically adjust the view to show all your dated events.
- **Manual Range**: Use the "From" and "To" date inputs to focus on a specific period.
- **Quick Ranges**: Use the preset buttons (e.g., "1800s", "±50yr") to quickly jump to specific timeframes.

## Lanes
- **Character Lanes**: Events are grouped by character. 
- **Reorder**: Drag the lane headers (using the `⋮⋮` handle) to reorder the character lanes.
- **Undated**: Events without a specific date appear in the "Undated Events" lane at the bottom.

## Interacting with Events
- **View Details**: Click on an event bar to open the event details modal.
